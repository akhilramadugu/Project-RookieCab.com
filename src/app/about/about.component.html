About RookieCab

Welcome to RookieCab, the dedicated platform for students on the move!

At RookieCab, we understand the unique transportation needs of students. Our mission is to provide a safe, affordable, and convenient ride-sharing solution that connects student riders with student drivers. Whether you're heading to class, returning home, or exploring new places, RookieCab is here to make your journey smoother.

For Student Riders:

Are you tired of waiting for public transport or paying hefty fares for traditional cabs? Are you getting sick of asking for rides on whatsapp groups? RookieCab is your go-to solution. Our platform allows you to find rides from fellow students who are wlling to give one. Not only does this make your travel more economical, but it also gives you a chance to meet new people and build connections within your student community.

For Student Drivers:

Do you have a car and want to offset your travel costs? With RookieCab, you can offer rides to other students and earn some extra cash. It's a great way to make the most of your empty seats while helping your peers get around. Plus, it's a fun way to meet other students and make new friends on your journeys.

Why RookieCab?

  • Student-Focused: Our platform is tailored specifically for students, ensuring that your transportation needs are met in the best possible way.
  • Economical: We have a tailored pricing scheme which is quite economical. Worried about middleman fees? Payment gateway charges? You pay directly to the rider.
  • Convenient: Easy to use, with real-time ride matching and scheduling.
  • Safe: It is for students, given by students. You are in good hands.

Join RookieCab today and be part of a growing network of students who are redefining the way they travel. Let's make every ride an opportunity to connect, share, and save!
